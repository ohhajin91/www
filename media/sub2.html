  • DO YOU WANT TO BUILD SNOW MAN

    "DO YOU WANT TO BUILD SNOW MAN" Anna is convincing Elsa to get out of her room so they can go play outside. However, when Anna was 15, she tried to convince Elsa to at least leave the room, as since their parents died, they have nothing but each other.

  • LOVE IS AN OPEN DOOR

    "LOVE IS AN OPEN DOOR" Performed after Anna explains her loneliness by being shut out from Elsa for years, with Hans promising to never shut Anna out. They ask to marry in the end.

  • LET IT GO

    "LET IT GO" Appears after an ostracized Elsa flees the kingdom of Arendelle soon after her ice powers are discovered by the public. Initially, Elsa is still distraught, but she realizes that she no longer needs to hide her powerful abilities anymore and declares herself free from the stress and fears that she has been faced with since childhood.

  • IN SUMMER

    "IN SUMMER" In this song, Olaf sings about how he would love to experience summer (obviously not realizing what would actually happen to him during the summer), while setting examples in his fantasy, such as sunbathing and sailing.
